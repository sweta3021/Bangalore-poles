About Us

Light is life. At Lumisol, we believe that lighting up our environment, our homes, our workplaces, our gardens, our roads and parking lots with a suitable source of light and technology is not just a mechanical, manufacturing and an installation job, but an art that when expressed to perfection, makes human life easier and enjoyable. All our products and services are built, fabricated and designed around this very philosophy.

Our Business started in the year 2001 and joined with entrepreneurs who believed in the same goal of creating a one-stop solution for all exterior lighting needs of the mid- and large scale industries, is today situated in the Silicon valley India, Bangalore.

Recognized by our customers and certification bodies for our unparalleled quality and extremely customized lighting solutions, we at Lumisol have become increasingly efficient in the art of lighting and Pole Fabrication. And we believe in sustainable, recyclable, zero-carbon footprint solutions in this regard. Our Solar Lighting systems that come together with low-energy consuming LED lights, a battery, a pole-mounted solar panel, a Dusk-to- Dawn automatic switch-on switch-off circuitry that is extremely low on maintenance is a proof of our concerns towards our environment.

Our wide range of lighting solutions include down light, flood light, bay light, poster poles, bollards, cob light etc. all powered by energy-efficient LED lighting.

Visit our products and services section here to have a look at all our offerings.

We also do street light poles including solar application of various model & light, pole / drawing or your imagination is all we need to offer you the pole what you require. All Bangalore poles Products Are 100% Indigenous "Make in India" Products I.e. Concept, Architecture, Design, Development, Manufacturing, Testing and Quality Control Are Done in Bangalore.

Our strength is the capability to produce new designs and innovate as per customer requirement.

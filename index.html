About Lumisol

We have established ourselves as a leading name of Lumisol. Lumisol represents unparalleled quality and professionalism in the area of lighting and pole fabrication.

Light is life. At Lumisol, we believe that lighting up our environment, our homes, our workplaces, our gardens, our roads and parking lots with a suitable source of light and technology is not just a mechanical, manufacturing and an installation job, but an art that when expressed to perfection, makes human life easier and enjoyable.

Lumisol, a company that was the brain-child of a few energetic entrepreneurs who believed in the same goal of creating a one-stop solution for all exterior lighting needs of the mid- and large scale industries, is today situated in India with its headquarters in the Silicon valley of India, Bangalore / Bengaluru. Recognized by our customers and certification bodies for our unparalleled quality and extremely customized lighting solutions, we at Lumisol have become increasingly efficient in the art of lighting and Pole Designs.
